The Experience

What to Expect

We’ve designed our Saturdays to be the highlight of your child’s week! It’s a perfect mix of creativity, guided learning, and lots of smiles as we build and program together.

Camp Details

  • Who: Junior Innovators (5–8) & High School Mentors
  • When: Joyful Saturday morning/afternoon sessions
  • Vibe: Relaxed, supportive, and highly interactive
  • Focus: Making friends while making robots!

What We Do

  • Creative Coding: Learning that programming is just another way to tell a story or solve a puzzle.
  • Hands-on Building: Snapping, wiring, and assembling robots together.
  • Show & Tell: Celebrating everyone's unique projects and cheering each other on.

A Typical Fun-Filled Saturday

  • Welcome & Warm-up: We gather around, share some laughs, and introduce the day's cool mission.
  • The "Build" Phase: Working in small teams, kids get their hands on hardware and start creating.
  • The "Code" Phase: We hop onto our devices to write the instructions that bring our creations to life.
  • Test & Tinker: Did the robot bump into the wall? Great! Let's figure out how to fix it together.
  • Wrap Up: High-fives, celebrating the day's successes, and looking forward to next week.
