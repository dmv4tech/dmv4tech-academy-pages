Intro to AI & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are shaping the world around us — from voice assistants to self-driving cars. At DMV4Tech, we simplify these exciting technologies for young minds!

What Kids Will Learn:

  • What is AI and how it works
  • Fun projects like teaching a computer to recognize animals
  • Basic logic and pattern recognition
  • Interactive games and hands-on AI tools like Google's Teachable Machine

This course sparks curiosity and encourages kids to think like data scientists and problem solvers — all in a fun and safe environment!
