Core Program

Robotics & Coding Lab

Watch your child's imagination come to life! In our supportive weekend labs, students discover how much fun it is to build physical robots and write the code that makes them move.

What They Will Discover

  • For the Little Ones (5–8): We focus on the joy of creating. Using block-based coding and child-friendly robotics, they learn early math and spatial logic without even realizing they're studying!
  • For our Teens: A safe place to stretch their skills. They'll dive into real-world programming languages (like Python) and build projects they can be truly proud of.
  • The Magic of Teamwork: No one learns in isolation. Our high schoolers gently mentor the younger kids, creating a beautiful cycle of community learning.
  • Creative Problem Solving: We celebrate mistakes as part of the learning process! Kids learn how to tinker, test, and try again with a smile.
