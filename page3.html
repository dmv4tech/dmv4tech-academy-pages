High School Track

Mentorship & Leadership Lab

We believe the absolute best way to learn something is to teach it. Our leadership program allows high schoolers to build incredible confidence by mentoring our younger students.

How Our Teens Grow

  • Empowering Others: Teens guide our 5-8 year olds through fun robotics builds, learning patience and empathy along the way.
  • Finding Their Voice: Explaining programming concepts to younger kids is the ultimate public speaking and communication practice!
  • Project Ownership: Teens get to lead small coding projects from start to finish, experiencing what it's like to be a true team leader.
  • A Welcoming Space: We provide a zero-pressure environment where teens can develop soft skills that will serve them for a lifetime, both in college and beyond.
