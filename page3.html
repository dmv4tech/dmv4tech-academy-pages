Robotics & Coding

Age Group: 8–14 years

Duration: 12 weeks (1 session/week)

Level: Beginner to Intermediate

What Kids Will Learn

  • Introduction to robotics components (sensors, motors, controllers)
  • Hands-on experience assembling robots
  • Programming robots to perform tasks
  • Basic AI and automation concepts

Tools Used

Micro:bit, Arduino, LEGO Mindstorms (depending on course track)
